History

Whitewater rafting has a rich history that dates back thousands of years. The earliest evidence of rafting can be traced to ancient civilizations such as the Egyptians and Mesopotamians. In the modern era, rafting became popular in the mid-20th

century and has since become a global adventure sport with advanced equipment and safety measures. Today, rafting is enjoyed by people of all ages around the world. It combines teamwork, nature, and adventure, making it one of the most exciting outdoor activities.
