History

Whitewater rafting has a rich history that dates back thousands of years. The earliest evidence of rafting can be traced to ancient civilizations such as the Egyptians and Mesopotamians. In the modern era, rafting became popular in the mid-20th century and has since become a global adventure sport with advanced equipment and safety measures.
